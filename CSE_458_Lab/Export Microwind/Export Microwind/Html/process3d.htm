Process Steps in 3D ()

Click the "3D icon" or "Simulation -> Process Steps in 3D" to see the 3-dimentionnal aspect of your circuit. Click "Next step" to watch how the layout currently edited on the screen will be fabricated using the selected technology. Use the arrow to shift the displayed portion. Zooms in and out are available.

In demo mode, the steps of the CMOS process are illustrated in sequence.
